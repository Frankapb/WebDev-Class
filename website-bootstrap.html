Services

One on One

Personal traning is called personal training because it's all about 'one on one' advice, guidance and motivation. The whole reason for paying for your own fitness trainer, is to get this attention and a training programme that's right for you.

Diet plans

A healthy lifestyle can be attained by maintaining a balanced diet and keeping into consideration to meet all the essential nutrients required by the body. A proper meal plan helps to attain ideal body weight and reduce the risk of chronic diseases.

Functional training

Functional training can improve the overall function of your body, boosting muscle strength and endurance. You can develop stability in your muscles and your body, allowing you to complete everyday activities more efficiently.These exercises typically use the whole body

Goals tracker

For us your goals should be specific as possible so as to be able to track progress. “I want to lose weight” or “I want to run faster” aren’t bad goals, but they’re just too general. “I want to run a half-marathon in under two hours” are specific goals that are easy to measure.

Fitness journey

Physical fitness is a state of health and well-being and, more specifically, the ability to perform aspects of sports, occupations and daily activities. Physical fitness is generally achieved through proper nutrition, moderate-vigorous physical exercise, and sufficient rest.
